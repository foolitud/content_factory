# Referral & Affiliate Program Strategy

## Referral vs. Affiliate: When to Use Each

### Customer Referral Programs
- **Referrer**: Existing customer
- **Motivation**: Social currency + rewards
- **Dynamics**: High trust, lower volume
- **Best For**: Word-of-mouth products, consumer apps

### Affiliate Programs
- **Referrer**: Content creators, influencers, partners
- **Motivation**: Revenue/commission
- **Dynamics**: Variable trust, high volume
- **Best For**: Higher-ticket products, SaaS

---

## Program Design

### The Referral Loop
1. **Trigger Moment**: When the user experiences value (Aha moment).
2. **Share Action**: Personalized link, in-product sharing.
3. **Conversion**: Frictionless landing page for the new user.
4. **Reward**: Double-sided incentives are most effective.

### Incentive Types
| Type | Best For |
|------|----------|
| Cash/Credit | Marketplaces, fintech |
| Product Credit | SaaS, recurring tools |
| Free Months | Subscriptions |
| Feature Unlock | Freemium models |

---

## Viral Modeling

**Viral Coefficient (K-factor)**: `K = Invitations × Conversion Rate`.
- `K > 1`: Exponential growth.
- `K < 1`: Amplified baseline growth.

---

## Fraud Prevention
- Email verification.
- Device fingerprinting.
- Payout delays (manual review for large rewards).
